Research projects

PDeeDyn : Predictive Demogenetics of the ecological and evolutionary Dynamics of freshwater and coastal populations

This project coordinated by Solenn STOECKEL and Eric PETIT has the aim of a better comprehension of biological invasions and other phenomena with rapid evolution and changes. My PhD forms part of this project and is about conception and study of demogenetic models to establish its strengths and limitations to simulate and infer demogenetic and eco-evolutionary processes. Applying this to real-worl data will provide new insights and support decision-making for the monitoring and management of invasions.
